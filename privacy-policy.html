Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Akari Flow ("Akari Flow," "the Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website at akariflow.com (the "Site") or otherwise interact with our business. This Privacy Policy describes the categories of information we collect, the purposes for which we use that information, and the choices available to you.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Site and to information collected in connection with our services. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that we do not own or control, even where those services are linked from or embedded within the Site.

Company contact information: Akari Flow, akariflowsolutions@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Information provided directly. When you request a consultation, submit a form, or otherwise contact us, we may collect your name, email address, telephone number, company name, and industry.
  • Scheduling information. The Site uses Calendly to enable appointment booking. Information submitted through the scheduling widget is collected and processed by Calendly in accordance with its own privacy policy, available at calendly.com/privacy.
  • Information collected automatically. When you access the Site, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you visit.
  • Third-party resources. The Site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers, which can disclose your IP address to Google.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To schedule, manage, and provide consultations and audits;
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support;
  • To send information regarding our services to individuals who have requested to receive such communications;
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the Site; and
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Site, and third-party tools embedded within it, including Calendly, may use cookies and similar technologies to operate correctly and to remember user preferences.

5. How We Disclose Information

We may disclose information to the following categories of recipients:

  • Service providers engaged to support our operations, including Calendly and our hosting provider;
  • Legal and regulatory authorities, where required by law or necessary to protect our rights; and
  • A successor entity, in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your state or country of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your personal information, and to opt out of certain processing activities. To exercise any applicable rights, please contact us at akariflowsolutions@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or for such longer period as may be required by applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for business owners and professionals and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16, where applicable under local law).

9. Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Effective Date" above reflects the date of the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site following any such changes constitutes acknowledgment of the revised Privacy Policy.
